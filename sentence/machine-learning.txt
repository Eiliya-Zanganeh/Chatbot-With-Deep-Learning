Hey there! Let's dive into the exciting world of machine learning! Machine learning is an application of artificial intelligence (AI) that enables systems to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. This learning happens through the use of algorithms that analyze data, recognize patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention. ### Types of Machine Learning: 1. Supervised Learning: This type of learning involves training a model on a labeled dataset, where the model learns to map input data to the correct output. 2. Unsupervised Learning: Here, the model is trained on unlabeled data with the goal of discovering hidden patterns or intrinsic structures. 3. Reinforcement Learning: In this setup, the algorithm learns to make decisions by interacting with an environment and receiving feedback in the form of rewards or penalties.
Hey there! Machine learning is like teaching your computer to learn from data and make decisions without being explicitly programmed for every scenario.
